Services

Act as a technology translator

A number of pre-existing solutions exist for technology challenges, but they are either marketed exclusively to other technology companies or are obscured behind industry specific jargon. I can help organizations find and implement these solutions, often for discounted rates or completely free of charge.

Tell stories with data

Organizing and making sense out of a giant mass of data can be intimidating. My skill set and experience enables me to efficiently store, organize, and analyze data. I enable nonprofit organizations to tell the story they want with the data they have.

Provide simple, sustainable, custom solutions

Sometimes a unique piece of code needs to be developed to overcome a particular technical challenge. When I create these solutions the goal is always to create something that the client can run on their own, for the long-term, and without expert intervention or maintenance. This can include providing training and documentation for a client's existing team to utilize.
